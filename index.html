The Crystal Collector Game

Match the Crystal Value to the Magic Number and WIN!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Crystal Value.

You win the game by matching your Crystal Value to the random number or Magic Number! You lose the game if your Crystal Value goes above the Magic Number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will change.
